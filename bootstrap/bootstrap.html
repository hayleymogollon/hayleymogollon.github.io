About

Hello! My name is Hayley Mogollon Coppel and I am currently a student at New York University studying Integrated Digital Media. I was born in New Port Beach, CA but I was raised in Cabo San Lucas, México. My teenage years were in San Diego, CA and I also studied abroad for a year in a school called Le Châtelard. My passions involve playing the piano, dancing, and filmmaking. I started doing these things when I was very young, and have not stopped doing them since. I have done many projects revolving around those passions of mine like composing my own songs on the piano, dancing in competitions, and making my school's promotion videos when I studied abroad. Enjoy this website where you can explore some of my life and work.
