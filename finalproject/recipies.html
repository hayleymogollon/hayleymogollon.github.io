Manzana Con Chile

Ingredients

  • Forrito
  • Apple
  • Miguelito powder (optional)

Method

  1. Place Forrito in the microwave for 10 seconds
  2. Take your softer Forrito and start covering your apple with it
  3. Once apple is completely covered in a smooth texture, add some Miguelito spicy powdered if desired
  4. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
